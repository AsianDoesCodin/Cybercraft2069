Server identity

A cyberpunk MMO server in Linefield City.

The modpack page describes Cybercraft2069 as an MMO cyberpunk experience. Players begin by doing gigs, choose from multiple jobs, upgrade transportation over time, rent apartments, and explore the city with friends.

The pack uses Minecraft 1.20.1 with Fabric. It includes TACZ guns, flying cars, Automobility cars, TV and advertisement boards, projectors, dancing Joi holograms, Nostalgia shaders, and Patrix texture.

City ladder

Ranks

Three mob-style rank tiers for players climbing from VIP status to the top of the city hierarchy.

Tier 01

VIP

The first premium step for players who want a sharper presence, smoother progression, and a more recognized place in the city.

  • Priority access to starter gigs and city-side delivery routes.
  • Extra apartment slot for players building a first foothold in the city.
  • Custom chat tag that marks VIP status across the server.
Tier 02

Boss

A stronger rank for players running bigger operations, with better movement, better returns, and more authority across the city.

  • Expanded home and warp options for faster movement between districts.
  • Improved job payout multiplier for players managing steady income routes.
  • Exclusive rank styling in chat and the live player list.
Tier 03

Kingpin

The top-tier rank for players who want the highest-status identity and the strongest sense of control over city life.

  • Highest transport convenience tier with expanded travel and property limits.
  • Priority queue placement during peak server hours.
  • Elite rank badge built for a clear top-of-the-food-chain presence.
